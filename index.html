So, you want to build a website...

...now what?

You're all signed up for a WordPress account,

you've picked out a theme and installed some plugins,

you've already tweeted that you're starting a blog...

It's a big decision.

  • The topic you choose will affect how you design your blog.
  • You want to stand out.
  • It's hard to narrow your blog down to just one thing.
  • "It's all been said/done before."
  • What do you have going on that others will want to read about?

Step #1

Identify some topics you could possibly write about.

Food For Thought

  • What makes you unique?
  • What are you passionate about?
  • What do you look forward to doing every day?
  • How do you spend your free time?
  • Do you have any talents?
  • Are you an expert in anything?
  • Do you have any projects you're working on?
  • Is there anything you've been meaning to learn?
  • In your social circles, are you the go-to person for certain types of advice?
  • Is there anything in your life that you'd like to document or reflect on later?

Step #2

Narrow your list down (or don't)

Most blogs fall into one of two main categories

General Blogs

  • Cover a range of topics (e.g. lifestyle blogs)
  • Larger audience, easier to gain traffic
  • More chances for advertising deals

But...

  • SEO is going to be difficult
  • Harder to build a loyal audience
  • Content might be unfocused (it's good to find a strong common theme)

Niche Blogs

  • Focus on one topic
  • Easier to gain a loyal following; readers know what to expect
  • You'll have better chances of ranking on Google
  • Targeted advertising opportunities

But...

  • You might feel cornered/limited
  • Readership will likely plateau due to limited amount of interest

Step #3

Check out the competition

Identify common design elements

  • What kinds of fonts and colors do they use?
  • How would you describe the overall feel of other websites?
  • What could you do differently so your blog will stand out?
  • Which elements should you copy so your blog looks like it belongs without plagiarizing?

Do some SEO super-sleuthing

  • Search Engine Optimization = strategies for obtaining a higher ranking in the search results page of a search engine.
  • Which keywords are other blogs using to rank?
  • How many results are returned for certain questions or phrases related to your niche?
  • Can you identify any keywords related to your topic(s) that other blogs aren't targetting?

Free SEO Tools:

  • Google Keyword Planner
  • Keyword Tool
  • All In One SEO Pack
  • Yoast SEO

Step #4

Start blogging!
